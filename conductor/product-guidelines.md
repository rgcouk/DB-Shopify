# Product Guidelines - DB-Shopify

## Voice and Tone
Concise, benefit-driven, and direct, optimized for SEO and accessibility while remaining user-friendly.

## Design Principles
- **Minimal Dark Aesthetics (Lux-Noir)**: Premium, sophisticated feel using dark themes and high-quality imagery.
- **Stylized Benefit Clarity**: Clearly communicating product benefits through elegant and intentional design.
- **High-Fidelity Brand Storytelling**: Using performance-optimized headless technology to tell the brand's story effectively.
