# Product Definition - DB-Shopify

## Project Name
DB-Shopify

## Project Description
A custom Shopify Hydrogen storefront for "Druid and Bear" brand.

## Problem Statement
Provides a premium, performance-optimized shopping experience for high-end wellness products.

## Target Users
Health-conscious consumers who prioritize high-performance wellness through wild-sourced, functional ingredients.

## Key Goals
- Maximize conversion rates for the core honey collection.
- Educate users on the scientific benefits of Lion's Mane, Reishi, and Cordyceps.
- Establish a premium "Lux-Noir" aesthetic that justifies a high price point.
